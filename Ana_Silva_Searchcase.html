05 · The Problem

The root problem was not catalog size. It was discoverability.

Customer problem

  • Discovery was limited to category silos.
  • Relevant products were hidden unless users knew where to look.
  • The experience reflected internal structure, not customer intent.

Business problem

  • Reduced exposure to the full ecosystem.
  • Fewer cross-category journeys.
  • Limited opportunities to create richer baskets.
06 · The Solution

We chose the smallest experiment capable of validating the hypothesis.

Navigation redesign
Holistic, but heavy. High investment and longer timeline.
→
Cross-sell modules
Useful, but only after a product is already discovered.
→
Unified Search
Fast learning. Cross-category by design. Clear signal of intent.

10 · The Solution · What I Made Different

I framed Search as a learning experiment, not a feature launch.

This helped the team start small, validate customer behavior, and create evidence for a broader product direction before investing in larger structural changes.

The mindset changed from “deliver Search” to “understand how customers discover products across the ecosystem.”
